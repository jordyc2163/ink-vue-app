Background

Welcome to my page. The thought behind this came from my fascination of art and the artform of tattooing. There are many talented artists in the world and many people wanting their masterpeice and appreciation as a permanent part of themselves. However you appreciate work, I noticed people struggle with finding artists, relying on word of mouth or hours of research. I hope to allow visitors the chance to explore tattoo artists all around the world, while viewing their work seamlessly, skipping the labor of research. Here I hope, whether as a customer or an artist, your favorite piece can come to fruition and recognition is in your art achieved respectively. Thank you for visiting.
